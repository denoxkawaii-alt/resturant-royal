About Us

A Legacy of Royal Flavor in the Heart of Dubai

Royal Spice Restaurant brings together the richness of Indian royal kitchens, the finesse of Chinese wok mastery, and the warmth of Continental comfort food — plated in an atmosphere of quiet, gilded luxury.

Every dish is prepared with premium, hand-selected ingredients, slow-cooked traditions, and a level of craft that turns a meal into an occasion. From our hand-tied naans to our dum-sealed biryanis, nothing leaves our kitchen without the chef's personal approval.

  • Best Quality, Every Single Plate
  • Fresh Ingredients Sourced Daily
  • An Unmatched Fine Dining Experience
